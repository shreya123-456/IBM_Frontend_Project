Journalism

Journalism is the practice of gathering, assessing, creating, and presenting news and information. It involves various processes such as investigating events, conducting interviews, verifying facts, and delivering stories in various formats, including articles, broadcasts, and digital media.

Courses

  1. Bachelor of Arts in Journalism(BA)

    Duration: 3 years

    Eligibility:

    • Candidates must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) education from a recognized board.

    • A minimum percentage in the 10+2 examination, usually around 50-60%, depending on the institution.

    • Some universities and colleges may require candidates to clear entrance exams specific to journalism or mass communication programs.

  2. Master of Science In Investigative Journalism

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • Candidates must have completed a bachelor's degree in journalism, mass communication, or a related field from a recognized university.

    • A minimum percentage in the bachelor's degree, usually around 50-60%, depending on the institution.

    • Some universities and colleges may require candidates to clear entrance exams specific to journalism or mass communication programs.

  3. Certifications For Digital Journalism

    • Certificate Programs by Indian Institutes

    • Online Certification Programs(Eg: edX,Udemy)

    • International Certifications(Eg: Poynter Institute,ICFJ)

Public Relations

Media Studies

Media Studies is an academic discipline that explores the content, history, and effects of various media, including print, digital, and electronic forms. It examines how media influences society, shapes public opinion, and affects cultural and social norms.The field often intersects with disciplines such as sociology, psychology, and political science, providing a comprehensive understanding of the media landscape and its impact on the world.

Courses

  1. Bachelor of Arts in Media Studies(BA)

    Duration: 3 years

    Eligibility:

    • Candidates must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) education from a recognized board.

    • Most institutions require a minimum percentage in the 10+2 examination, usually around 50-60%.

    • Delhi University Entrance Test (DUET) for certain colleges under the University of Delhi.

    • Jamia Millia Islamia Entrance Test for its media programs.

    • Some institutions may consider scores from national or state-level entrance exams related to mass communication or media studies.

  2. Master of Arts in Media and Communication

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • Candidates usually need a bachelor's degree in communication, media studies, journalism, or a related field from a recognized university.

    • A minimum percentage in the bachelor's degree, usually around 50-60%, depending on the institution.

    • Some universities or colleges might require candidates to pass an entrance exam or undergo a personal interview as part of the admission process.

  3. Certifications for Digital Media Production

    • Google Digital Garage - Fundamentals of Digital Marketing (IABC)

    • Coursera - Digital Media and Marketing Strategies by the University of Illinois

    • edX - Digital Media Production by RIT (Rochester Institute of Technology)

Film and Television

A Film and Television course focuses on the art and industry of creating and producing visual media content. It covers various aspects of filmmaking and television production, including scriptwriting, directing, cinematography, editing, and sound design.The course often includes hands-on experience with equipment and software, providing practical skills for careers in film and television. Additionally, students explore industry trends and develop creative and technical competencies essential for successful careers in media.

Courses

  1. Bachelor of Fine Arts in Film Production(BA)

    Duration: 3-4 years

    Eligibility:

    • Candidates must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) education from a recognized board.

    • Most institutions require a minimum percentage in the 10+2 examination, usually around 50-60%.

    • Film and Television Institute of India (FTII), Pune: Conducts an entrance exam and interview for its programs.

    • Satyajit Ray Film and Television Institute (SRFTI), Kolkata: Conducts an entrance exam and interview.

  2. Master of Fine Arts in Screenwriting

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • Candidates usually need a bachelor's degree in any field from a recognized university. A background in writing, literature, film, or media can be advantageous.

    • A minimum percentage in the bachelor's degree, usually around 50-60%, depending on the institution.

    • Most programs require candidates to submit a portfolio of their writing, which may include scripts, short stories, or other relevant work.

    • Some institutions may conduct interviews or require candidates to pass a written test as part of the admission process.

  3. Certifications For Cinematography

    • Certified Professional Cinematographer (CPC) by the International Cinematographers Guild (ICG)

    • Cinematography Certificate by the New York Film Academy (NYFA)

    • Cinematography Certification by the American Film Institute (AFI)

    • Digital Cinematography Certification by the International School of Film and Television (ISFT)

    • Film and Television Production Certificate by Coursera (offered in partnership with institutions such as the University of London)

Advertising

Advertising is a strategic communication process aimed at promoting products, services, or ideas to a targeted audience through various media channels. It involves creating compelling messages and visuals to capture attention, generate interest, and drive consumer action. Advertising strategies often include market research, campaign development, media planning, and performance analysis to ensure effectiveness and reach. The field encompasses diverse platforms such as television, radio, print, digital media, and outdoor advertising, with the goal of influencing consumer behavior and building brand awareness.
