Entrepreneurship and Innovation

The Business and Management domain covers a broad spectrum of subjects focused on understanding, managing, and leading business enterprises. This domain equips students with essential skills in administration, strategic planning, and operational management, making them well-suited for leadership roles in various organizations.

Courses

  1. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Entrepreneurship

    Duration: 3 years

    Eligibility:

    • Completion of 10+2 with a minimum percentage (e.g., 50%).

    • Some institutions require a score in entrance exams like JEE or equivalent.

    • English proficiency (some colleges may require an English test).

    • Minimum age requirement (often 17 years and above).

  2. Master of Science in Innovation Management

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Management, or a related field

    • Relevant work experience (often 1-2 years).

    • Good academic record with a minimum percentage (e.g., 50%).

  3. Certificate in Startup Management

    Duration: 6 months to 1 year

    Eligibility:

    • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent.

    • Some programs may accept candidates with significant professional experience.

    • Basic understanding of business principles.

    • Letter of recommendation (may be required for some programs).

Salary Expectations

  • Entry-Level:₹5,00,000 - ₹8,00,000 per annum

  • Mid-Level: ₹10,00,000 - ₹15,00,000 per annum

  • Senior-Level:₹20,00,000 - ₹40,00,000+ per annum
