Performing arts

Performance arts encompass a range of artistic disciplines where artists use their voices, bodies, or inanimate objects to convey artistic expression. This category includes theater, dance, music, opera, mime, puppetry, and circus arts, among others. These forms often combine elements of storytelling, movement, sound, and visual art to create immersive experiences. Performance arts can be live or recorded and are often presented in theaters, concert halls, streets, and other venues. They play a crucial role in culture by reflecting societal values, provoking thought, and providing entertainment. Performance arts also foster creativity and community engagement.

Fashion design

Fashion design is the art of applying design, aesthetics, and natural beauty to clothing and accessories. It is influenced by cultural and social attitudes and evolves over time. Fashion designers work in a variety of ways, designing clothing and accessories. They create original garments, or adapt fashion trends. They must consider who is likely to wear a garment and the situations in which it will be worn. Designers use a wide range of materials and techniques, including computer-aided design (CAD). Fashion design encompasses both haute couture and ready-to-wear collections, blending creativity with functionality and market demand.
