Public Health

Public Health is the science and art of preventing disease, prolonging life, and promoting health through organized efforts and informed choices of society, organizations, public and private, communities, and individuals.

Courses

  1. Bachelor of Public Health (BPH)

    Duration: 3-4 years

    Eligibility:

    • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent examination with Biology as a compulsory subject.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 50% in 10+2 (may vary by institution).

  2. Master of Public Health (MPH)

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • A recognized Bachelor’s degree in any health-related field.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 50% (may vary by institution).

  3. Ph.D. in Public Health

    Duration: 3-5 years

    Eligibility:

    • A Master’s degree in Public Health or related field.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 55% (may vary by institution).

Current Trends

  • Emphasis on epidemiology and biostatistics

  • Focus on health policy and management

  • Increasing importance of global health initiatives

Expected Salary

  • Freshers: ₹3-5 lakhs per annum

  • Experienced: ₹8-15 lakhs per annum

Top Institutes

  • AIIMS, New Delhi

  • Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai

  • Public Health Foundation of India

Health Informatics

Health Informatics is a field that combines health care, information technology, and business administration to improve the quality and efficiency of health care delivery through data management and analytics.

Courses

  1. Bachelor of Science in Health Informatics

    Duration: 3 years

    Eligibility:

    • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent examination with Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 50% in 10+2 (may vary by institution).

  2. Master of Science in Health Informatics

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • A recognized Bachelor’s degree in Health Informatics or related field.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 50% (may vary by institution).

  3. Ph.D. in Health Informatics

    Duration: 3-5 years

    Eligibility:

    • A Master’s degree in Health Informatics or related field.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 55% (may vary by institution).

Expected Salary

  • Freshers (0-2 years of experience): ₹5 to ₹8 lakhs per annum

  • Mid-Level Professionals (3-7 years of experience): ₹9 to ₹18 lakhs per annum

  • Experienced Professionals (8+ years of experience): ₹18 to ₹35 lakhs per annum or higher

Top Institutes

  • Shiv Nadar University

  • Indian Institute of Technology Madras

  • Jawaharlal Nehru University

Telehealth

Telehealth is the use of telecommunications technology to provide healthcare services remotely. It includes telemedicine, teletherapy, and remote patient monitoring, enhancing access to healthcare.

Courses

  1. Certificate in Telehealth

    Duration: 6 months

    Eligibility:

    • Open to healthcare professionals and students.

    • Completion of relevant basic healthcare or IT courses may be required.

  2. Diploma in Telehealth

    Duration: 1 year

    Eligibility:

    • A recognized Bachelor’s degree in healthcare or IT field.

    • Experience in healthcare preferred.

  3. Master of Telehealth

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • A recognized Bachelor’s degree in Healthcare, IT, or related field.

    • Relevant work experience may be required.

Current Trends

  • Big data analytics in healthcare

  • Telehealth and mobile health technologies

  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning applications

Expected Salary

  • Freshers (0-2 years of experience): ₹5 to ₹8 lakhs per annum

  • Mid-Level Professionals (3-7 years of experience): ₹9 to ₹18 lakhs per annum

  • Experienced Professionals (8+ years of experience): ₹18 to ₹35 lakhs per annum or higher

Top Institutes

  • AIIMS, Delhi

  • Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology

  • JIPMER, Puducherry

Genetic Counseling

Genetic Counseling involves evaluating family histories to assess the risk of inherited conditions, providing information about genetic disorders, and supporting families in making informed decisions.

Courses

  1. Master of Science in Genetic Counseling

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • A recognized Bachelor’s degree in Genetics, Biology, or related field.

    • Relevant experience in a healthcare setting may be required.

  2. Ph.D. in Genetic Counseling

    Duration: 3-5 years

    Eligibility:

    • A Master’s degree in Genetic Counseling or related field.

    • Relevant research experience may be required.

Current Trends

  • Advances in genomic technology

  • Personalized medicine

  • Increased public awareness of genetic counseling

Expected Salary

  • Freshers (0-2 years of experience): ₹4 to ₹6 lakhs per annum

  • Mid-Level Professionals (3-7 years of experience): ₹7 to ₹14 lakhs per annum

  • Experienced Professionals (8+ years of experience): ₹15 to ₹28 lakhs per annum or higher

Top Institutes

  • AIIMS, Delhi

  • Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology

  • Manipal Academy of Higher Education

Nursing

Nursing is a healthcare profession focused on the care of individuals, families, and communities to attain, maintain, or recover optimal health and quality of life. It includes various levels of education and specialization.

Courses

  1. Basic Nursing Certificate

    Duration: 1 year

    Eligibility:

    • Completion of high school education.

    • Completion of relevant health and biology courses.

  2. Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)

    Duration: 4 years

    Eligibility:

    • Completion of high school education with a focus on science subjects.

    • Minimum aggregate marks of 50% in high school (may vary by institution).

  3. Master of Science in Nursing (MSN)

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility:

    • A recognized Bachelor’s degree in Nursing.

    • Relevant work experience in healthcare may be required.

  4. Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP)

    Duration: 3-4 years

    Eligibility:

    • A Master’s degree in Nursing.

    • Relevant clinical experience and research may be required.

Expected Salary

  • Freshers (0-2 years of experience): ₹3 to ₹5 lakhs per annum

  • Mid-Level Professionals (3-7 years of experience): ₹5.5 to ₹10 lakhs per annum

  • Experienced Professionals (8+ years of experience): ₹10 to ₹20 lakhs per annum or higher

Top Institutes

  • AIIMS, Delhi

  • CMC, Vellore

  • JIPMER, Puducherry
